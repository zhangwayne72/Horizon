This bill could disrupt Mercedes-Benz&\#x27;s US operations and set a precedent for restricting foreign-owned connected vehicles. It highlights growing geopolitical tensions in the automotive and tech sectors. The bill is still under congressional review and has not yet become law. The 15% threshold applies to any foreign entity deemed a risk, with Chinese ownership specifically targeted.

The article notes that while many startups pursued novel hardware, the smartphone remains the most accessible and capable AI interface, with powerful chips, sensors, and app ecosystems already in place.
